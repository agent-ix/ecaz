# Review: Task 43 Safety Campaign Tracker (packet 006)

canonical source of truth for Task 43 completion. The tracker codifies:
campaign rules (no vacuous criteria, prefer real production helpers, no

- **All five prior reviewer findings are represented as tracker rows.**
  Many-seeds emptiness → G2. Strategy-named coverage gaps → G3.
  Row-independent parser → G4. SPIRE vacuum → G5. Cargo-careful

  closeout review of packet 005.

- **G8 (final audit) is the only remaining gate.** Tracker is explicit
  that the audit packet must come *after* G2–G7 are complete, which is
  the right ordering.

## Verdict

Approve. The tracker is the right artifact for managing a safety
campaign of this size and correctly answers the bar set in the prior
closeout review.
