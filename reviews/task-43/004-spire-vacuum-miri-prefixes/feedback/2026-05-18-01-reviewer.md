# Review: Task 43 SPIRE And Vacuum Miri Prefixes (packet 004)

## Summary

Promotes four existing pure tests into the `miri_` prefix:

- DiskANN vacuum `repair_neighbors` compaction and padding
  (`miri_vc_006_*`).
- DiskANN vacuum repair encoded-length preservation
  (`miri_vc_009_*`).
- SPIRE bounded vec-id dedupe in routed-leaf ranking.

## Confirmations

- **All four tests pass under Miri.** Logs are packet-local.
- **The DiskANN vacuum picks are the right pair.**
  `repair_neighbors` is the most lifetime-sensitive helper in the
  vacuum module (neighbor-slot byte slice juggling). Compaction +
  encoded-length preservation cover both the structural and the
  length contracts.
- **The SPIRE candidate picks cover both the producer and the
  consumer side.** Bounded ranking dedupe (producer) and cursor
  ranked emission (consumer) exercise both ends of the top-k
  pipeline.

## Findings

- **Vacuum coverage stops at `repair_neighbors`.** The strategy
  packet's DiskANN pure-vacuum list also named `mark_deleted`,
  `strip_dead_primary_heaptid`, and `is_fully_dead`. None of those got
  `miri_` prefixes in this packet. Each is short and pure; promotion
  cost would be minimal. Leaving them outside the Miri lane is a
  scope reduction that the closeout packet (005) does not appear to
  address either.
- **SPIRE vacuum module is entirely outside Miri.** The strategy
  noted `collect_visible_assignments` and delete-delta grouping as
  candidates if mock object-store tests could stay pure. This packet
  does not include them; the closeout does not either. SPIRE-side
  vacuum has zero `miri_` coverage as of this checkpoint.
- **Top-k coverage is narrow.** Strategy named
  `SpireScoredCandidateAccumulator`, `rank_bounded_scored_candidates`,
  `scored_candidate_cmp`, `rerank_scored_candidates_by_ip`, and
  `SpireScanCandidateCursor`. This packet covers the first via the
  routed-leaf dedupe test, and the cursor via the ranked-emission
  test. `scored_candidate_cmp` (the comparator that everything else
  depends on) and `rerank_scored_candidates_by_ip` (a hot path) are
  not in the Miri lane.

## Verdict

Approve as a representative slice. Tests promoted are pure, pass
under Miri, and cover the two highest-risk DiskANN vacuum contracts.
The reservation is that several explicit strategy candidates
(`mark_deleted`, `is_fully_dead`, `scored_candidate_cmp`,
`rerank_scored_candidates_by_ip`, SPIRE vacuum helpers) did not
receive Miri prefixes in this or the closeout packet. Recommend a
follow-up that closes those.
