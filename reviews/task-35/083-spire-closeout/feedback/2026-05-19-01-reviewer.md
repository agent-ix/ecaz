## Why this matters

This is the artifact that converts ~37 individual reviewer
approvals into a single auditable safety statement for SPIRE. A
future reviewer (human or agent) coming to SPIRE cold can:

1. Read the invariant summary (2 minutes).
2. Understand the active-epoch chain, lock/WAL scope, and guard
   inventory.
3. Treat every per-file SAFETY comment as an *instance* of those
   invariants rather than an independent claim.

That collapses the audit surface from 870 per-call invariants down
to ~10 cross-cutting invariants + per-call instance checks. **That
is what "real protection" looks like at the documentation layer**
— it converts the safety comments from prose into a structure with
load-bearing top-level facts.

It also makes the Task 50 structural work concretely actionable: the
five deferred candidates have specific names, specific surfaces,
and specific reviewer-corroborated need.
