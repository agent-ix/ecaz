# Reviewer Feedback — 143 HNSW Insert-Rewrite Page-Tuple Byte View Scope

Verdict: ⚠️ **concerns, non-blocking**.

## Confirmed

- `with_writable_page_tuple_bytes` (`src/am/ec_hnsw/insert.rs:1210–1241`)
  centralizes lookup, bounds check, and copy-target acquisition; backlink +
  scalar/V3/PqFastScan duplicate coalescing all flow through it.

## Concerns (non-blocking)

- **Aliasing pedantry.** The helper hands the closure both `*mut u8`
  (`tuple_ptr`) and `&[u8]` (`tuple_bytes`) over the same memory. Current
  callers decode the `&[u8]` first, then encode+copy back through
  `*mut u8` (no overlap in time), so behaviorally safe. Strict Rust
  aliasing forbids holding a shared borrow while writing through a raw
  pointer to the same bytes; `cargo miri` could flag this. Recommend a
  follow-up packet to split into read-only / writable variants.
- 1:1 duplication of `vacuum.rs::with_writable_vacuum_tuple_bytes` (144) —
  unify into one helper in `shared.rs`.
