## Answers to the packet's review questions

- **Right precursor before AM scan invokes production remote
  candidate receive?** Yes. AM scan needs to know "which PIDs go
  to which remote nodes" before it can populate the executor's
  `Planned` dispatches. Coupling that knowledge to local
  object-store reads (the existing routed-row helper) would force
  every remote leaf to also exist as a local object, which is
  exactly the multi-instance assumption Phase 11 is built to
  remove.
- **Right boundary to skip non-local placements during coordinator
  routing metadata load?** Yes. The coordinator-local loader's
  job is to find the best leaf PIDs for the query; whether those
  PIDs live locally or remotely is a placement question that
  belongs to the executor, not the routing planner. Splitting
  these is the right shape for AM scan integration.
- **Fixture proves remote leaf payloads are not read locally
  while preserving routed-row behavior?** Yes. The
  invalid-for-local-read placement plus a passing PID collection
  is a clean negative-positive pair that locks the invariant.
