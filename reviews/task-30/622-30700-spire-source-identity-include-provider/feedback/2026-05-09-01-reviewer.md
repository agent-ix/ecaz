  bytea widths, missing identity column under the reloption, multi-key
  index, partial index, and expression identity are all rejected

  required posture for cross-node dedupe correctness.

  mismatch). The threading goes through both populated build,
  empty-index insert bootstrap, live insert deltas, and boundary
  assignment paths.

  invariant "selected leaf PIDs may yield leaf-derived delta PIDs"
  preserves the selected-leaf endpoint model while letting live insert
  delta rows be returned. Documented in the receive-contract test.

  source-identity threading and replica-share proofs are explicitly
