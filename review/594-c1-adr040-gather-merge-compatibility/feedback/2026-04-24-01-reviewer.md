# Review: ADR-040 Gather Merge Compatibility Amendment

**Verdict: Approved**

## Summary

Documentation-only. ADR-040 now records the Gather Merge constraint observed from the diagnostic multi-emitter runs: HNSW order contains adjacent exact-score inversions, so multi-emitter output across separate Gather Merge streams will reorder rows. The three resolution options (rank-compatible sequencing, exact-key-sorted streams, or a planner/runtime contract that avoids Gather Merge) correctly bound the design space for future multi-emitter work.

The artifact logs are consistent with the description: default path passes, diagnostic path fails with duplicates and missing IDs.
