## TL;DR for the agent

**Merged correctly.** Adding `selected_result_slot_index` and
`selected_result_score_bits` to the coordinator header (with a valid
flag) is the right seam — callers can now read the current best
staged result without rescanning the slot array.

## Verification performed

- `EcParallelCoordinatorState` carries `selected_result_slot_index:
  AtomicU32` + `selected_result_score_bits: AtomicU32`
  (`src/am/common/parallel.rs:38–39`).
- `EC_PARALLEL_COORDINATOR_SELECTED_RESULT_VALID` flag gates both
  fields together (`parallel.rs:87`) — reads in
  `load_coordinator_snapshot` (`parallel.rs:656–670`) correctly
  surface `Option<u32>` / `Option<f32>` from the flag.
- Refresh is wired into publish, clear, release-via-clear, and
  rescan layout reset. Confirmed by inspecting the callers of
  `refresh_coordinator_selection_snapshot` — all four paths fire it.

## Findings

### 1. Flag-gated Option pattern is clean

**Severity:** Informational (positive)

Using `EC_PARALLEL_COORDINATOR_SELECTED_RESULT_VALID` to gate two
independent atomic fields into a single `Option<(slot_index, score)>`
is the right idiom for this. It keeps the shared header compact while
avoiding ambiguity about "is `selected_result_slot_index == u32::MAX`
the sentinel or a real value?" Keep this pattern for future paired
fields.

### 2. Score read can observe a torn load vs. slot_index

**Severity:** Low
**File:** `src/am/common/parallel.rs:656–670`

`load_coordinator_snapshot` loads `flags`, then under the gated flag
loads `selected_result_slot_index` and `selected_result_score_bits`
as independent atomics. A concurrent refresh between the two loads
could yield slot_index from generation N and score from generation
N+1. Today `amcanparallel = false` means no concurrent refresh is
possible, and the fast-path read in 474 re-validates against the
slot itself. Not a bug today; flagging for the concurrency slice.
