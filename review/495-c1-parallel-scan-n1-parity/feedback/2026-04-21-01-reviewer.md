## Findings

### 1. Bug is load-bearing, not cosmetic

The refill gap could silently drop graph rows on `n=1` parallel-bound
scans — the same binding that will be the first-enabled
`amcanparallel` path. Catching it here is the right forcing
function.

### 2. Parity harness picks the right streams

Both `(heap_tid, score)` and
`(heap_tid, approx_score, comparison_score, approx_rank)` parity
are pinned. PqFastScan's four-field stream catches rank-propagation
regressions that a pure score check would miss.
