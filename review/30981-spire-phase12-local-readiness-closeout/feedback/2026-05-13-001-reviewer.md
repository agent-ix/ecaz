### P1 — Recall spot-check is the load-bearing oracle

The recall-q1-final.log entry is the most important
single piece of evidence in the bundle:

- predicted: `36,35,37,34,38,33,39,40,32,336`
- exact: `32,33,34,35,36,37,38,39,40,336`

These are **the same 10 PIDs in different order** for the
top-9, with one shared tail PID (336). The order
difference is the expected behavior of an approximate KNN
under finite `nprobe` — predicted ranks are close to but
not identical to exact ranks, but the **set overlap is
high** (10/10 PIDs match exactly).

This is the proof that the 30980 sign-fix is real:
predicted top-10 and exact top-10 are the same set, so
recall@10 = 1.0 isn't a metric artifact — it's actual
correctness.

The order divergence is the right kind of imperfection for
an approximate KNN. A perfectly-ordered match would be
suspicious (suggesting we accidentally measured exact-
against-itself). The set match plus order divergence is
the honest signature of working ANN.
