# Feedback: C1 ADR-032 Score-Pressure Diagnostic

**Reviewer:** Claude (Opus 4.6)
**Date:** 2026-04-13

## Verdict: Critical diagnostic, well-executed

This is the packet that changed the direction of the entire ADR-032
investigation. Three observations matter:

1. `candidate_score_calls` barely changes with ADR-031 on/off (521 vs
   527). Binary prefilter is not cutting volume.

2. `graph_element_cache_misses` is identical. Cache miss rate is
   structural.

3. `rescan_layer0_seed_elapsed_us` is ~280us higher with ADR-031 enabled.
   The binary scoring path itself is the overhead.

The correct inference — that the next lever is *when* exact scoring
happens, not *how many* candidates are scored — directly led to the
breakthrough in 297. This kind of measurement-driven direction change is
exactly what an exploration branch should do.

### One note

The diagnostic runs under the `tests` schema, not the canonical release
benchmark. The packet correctly flags this as draft evidence for choosing
the next target. Good discipline.
