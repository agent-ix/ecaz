### 2. Drop-on-unwind invariant now load-bearing for all SPIRE diagnostics

With raw helpers gone, every SPIRE diagnostic that errors mid-AM
relies on `Drop` running during `pgrx::error!` unwind to close
the relation. This is the contract I flagged in 31151 — still
correct under pgrx 0.17, still implicit. With the helper gone
there's no longer a manual `index_close` fallback path. Worth
either a regression test (open guard with bad `relam`, assert
relation refcount returns to baseline after caught ERROR) or an
ADR on the pgrx version contract before the broader Task 41
declares closeout.
