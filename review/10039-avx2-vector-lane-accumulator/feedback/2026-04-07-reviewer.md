# Review Feedback: AVX2 Vector Lane Accumulator

**Verdict: Approved**

## Change shape

This is a clean micro-optimization: accumulate MSE and QJL contributions in `__m256` registers
across the full loop, then do one horizontal reduction at the end. Previously the AVX2 path was
storing-to-array and summing in scalar on every 8-lane chunk, which was the obvious next bottleneck
after the 3-bit decode specialization.

18% improvement on `score_ip_encoded` and the win holds at 20k iterations — not a noisy sample.

## Equivalence

The accumulator shape is obviously equivalent: addition is associative for f32 within the precision
guarantees we care about here (and the 1e-6 relative tolerance in the equivalence test already
accounts for reordering). The scalar path is untouched.

## Checkpoint granularity

This is narrow enough for its own checkpoint. One register-level change, one benchmark delta,
clear before/after. Good packet structure.
