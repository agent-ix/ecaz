## Findings

### 1. Drop path no longer hides admission losers

Before this slice, linear fallback could emit locally a row that
had already lost shared admission. That amounted to a silent
bypass around the merge seam. `DropAndContinue` removes it.

### 2. Retained blocker clears cleanly on drop

`retained_parallel_owned_output_blocker = None` at scan.rs:3411
means the 507 retry machinery won't mistake an admission-window
loser for a foreign-owner blocker that should be retained across
retries. Correct coupling.
