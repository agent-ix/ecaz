## Summary

Reviews 96-155 represent a long sequence of incremental scan refactoring slices. Each slice makes one structural change, validates it with tests, and commits. The overall direction is correct: the scan runtime is being transformed from a mixed bootstrap/linear dual-path executor into a unified staged result pipeline with explicit phase dispatch, shared materialization, and owned result state.

The refactoring is clearly building toward A3 (wire graph traversal into scan). Every slice removes one asymmetry between bootstrap and linear paths, making it easier to eventually replace both with a single beam-search-driven result buffer.
