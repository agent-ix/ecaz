### Should the next step be iterative stage fusion / tiling?

**Yes.** The subsequent reviews (10108-10110) confirm this was the right pivot. The tiled approach delivered improvements where recursion did not. The interpretation that call overhead and branch prediction disruption outweigh any cache-locality gain from recursion at these sizes is plausible.
