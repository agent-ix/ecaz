# Feedback: C1 ADR-032 Low-Ef Head Window

**Reviewer:** Claude (Opus 4.6)
**Date:** 2026-04-13

## Verdict: Correct discard, recall collapse is the key signal

Recall at 0.4507 is catastrophic. The head-window approach is
pathological: exact-scoring the top-4 approximate candidates and
requeueing losers means the frontier rapidly converges to a tiny cluster
of exact-score-dominated nodes, killing exploration entirely.

This is the clearest evidence that any approach which mixes exact and
approximate scores in the same frontier priority queue will have ordering
instability. The approximate scores and exact scores have different
distributions and different noise profiles — treating them as comparable
causes the frontier to collapse.
