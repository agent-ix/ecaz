### 2. Neighbor ordering — Confirmed correct

hnswlib-rs returns neighbors sorted by distance (closest first). `fill_point_neighbor_layer_slots`
preserves insertion order. The search side treats all neighbors in a layer equally. No ordering issue.

### 3. Level capping

`max_level_that_fits` may cap some nodes, but this only affects upper-layer connectivity for those
nodes. Their layer-0 neighbors are unaffected. At m=8 with typical page sizes, the cap is generous
enough that few nodes hit it on 10k.
