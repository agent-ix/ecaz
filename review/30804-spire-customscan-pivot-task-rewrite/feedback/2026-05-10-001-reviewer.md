## Findings

### P3 — Step 1 (executor-boundary preflight) is still phrased as a
prerequisite

Item 1 of the new ordered list ("C4/C5 executor-boundary preflight") is
inherited from the AM-era plan but re-scoped to "before CustomScan
depends on it." That's correct, but the in-line checklist items below it
still talk about "AM-boundary" in a couple of places that the diff did
not touch. Quick scan-and-rewrite pass would make the rewrite fully
internally consistent — not a blocker.

### P3 — Cleanup item phrasing on "dead AM cursor references"

The cleanup bullet `Remove dead AM cursor references to
requires_remote_row_materialization` says "the local-only AM path must
keep its classifier logic but no longer reference the superseded
materialization catalog." The local-only AM path was never supposed to
trigger `requires_remote_row_materialization` at all (no remote
placements ⇒ no materialization gate). Worth tightening the wording to
say the cleanup removes the AM-cursor branch that *would have* required
materialization on an active-remote-placement index, since CustomScan
now owns that path. As written it could be misread as removing
materialization gating from the local AM, which would re-open the
gate-bypass risk Stage A worked to close.

## Reviewer recommendation

Accept. P3 items are docs-only polish for the next docs touch on the
task file; not worth a re-spin of this packet.
