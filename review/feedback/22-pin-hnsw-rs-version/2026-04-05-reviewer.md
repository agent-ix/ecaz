## Answers to review questions

1. **Pinning level**: Exact `"0.3.4"` is appropriate for this project. `hnsw_rs` is only used for build-time graph construction (per ADR-002), and a breaking change in the graph topology output could silently affect index quality. Pinning exact and upgrading deliberately is the right call.

2. **Cargo.lock follow-up**: If `Cargo.lock` is already committed and resolves to `0.3.4`, no follow-up is needed. The manifest change ensures `cargo update` won't silently pull a different version.

3. **Other overly broad dependency specs**: `hex = "0.4"`, `rand = "0.8"`, `rand_chacha = "0.3"` are all semver-ranged, which is standard for utility crates. These don't carry the same risk as `hnsw_rs` since they don't affect index correctness. No action needed.
