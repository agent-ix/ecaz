## Acceptance

The Phase 9 closeout requirement that "each item has a baseline reference,
rationale, and concrete revisit conditions" is satisfied:

- ADR-060 — anisotropic centroid scoring: revisits on real50k or larger
  fixture with baseline recall@10 < ~0.95, on a hard-query subset of
  real10k under the same threshold, or on a new lane that exposes
  centroid-assignment false negatives. Reopening must record same-knobs
  baseline + treatment recall/latency, loss choice, interactions with
  other controls, and a default-off control surface.
- ADR-061 — IMI reshape: revisits on real50k+ fixture or a storage
  benchmark that shows single-IVF as the dominant limit. Reopening must
  cover load, storage, explain, latency, and recall lanes for both formats
  with a default-off control and a migration path.
- ADR-062 — query difficulty estimator: revisits when adaptive-`nprobe`
  diagnostics show a persistent failure mode (high false-reduction rate or
  kept-wide-with-no-benefit) or when a larger local fixture exposes
  meaningful per-query difficulty variance. Reopening must reference
  ADR-052/ADR-053, define estimator inputs/fallback, and ship A/B
  measurements.
